About Us

'A Series of Fortunate Events' is a event rental company located in Seattle, Washington that services weddings throughout the Seattle area. This company has been part of the wedding industry since 2007 and specializes in providing full-service event rental items that include everything you need from start to finish.

Products Offered

We offer high-quality service options to each couple. The professional sales and event staff will work closely with you to ensure the creation of your dream wedding. Whether you're thinking of an intimate outdoor wedding or an elaborate black-tie gala, we will use their experience to create an unforgettable event for you and your guests. Some, but not all, of the items offered include:

  • Aisle runners
  • Centerpieces
  • Dance floor
  • Lounge furniture
  • Tent accessories
  • Decor
  • Drapery
  • Lights
